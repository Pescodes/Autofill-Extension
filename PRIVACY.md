# Privacy Policy for Alt+P Autofill

**Effective Date:** February 7, 2026

Alt+P Autofill is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Use
**Alt+P Autofill does not collect or store any user data on external servers.** 

**Local Storage:** All profile information you enter (name, email, social media handles, etc.) is stored locally on your device or within your personal Google account's synchronized storage via the `chrome.storage.sync` API.
* **No Transmission:** Your data is never transmitted to the developer or any third-party servers. It remains entirely under your control and is used solely to provide the autofill functionality.

## 2. Permissions and Purpose
Our extension requests the following permissions to function:
* **Storage:** To save your profile information for later use.
* **ContextMenus:** To allow you to right-click and fill fields.
* **ActiveTab & Scripting:** To inject the saved data into the specific form fields you choose to fill.

## 3. Data Retention and Deletion
You have full control over your data:
* **Manual Deletion:** You can clear all saved data at any time using the "Clear" button within the extension popup.
* **Uninstallation:** If you uninstall the extension, all locally stored data associated with it will be deleted by the browser.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. Your data is not shared with any third-party services.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here on this GitHub page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub Issues page](https://github.com/Pescodes/Autofill-Extension/issues).
